Thunderbeam-Lightbeam for Chrome
Thanks for downloading Thunderbeam!

1. Open the Thunderbeam tab by clicking the Thunderbeam logo in the browser toolbar.
	 Thunderbeam starts recording connections as soon as it's installed.

2. To start visualizing your online interactions, open a new tab, navigate to a site, and then check back to the Thunderbeam tab.
	 Thunderbeam is used for our research study, thus your browser data might be used for research. But all data would be hashed and timely deletion for GDPR.
	 The extension developed from Lightbeam for Firefox (https://github.com/mozilla/lightbeam-we), but it had been stopped.

3. Thunderbeam is under development. If you find a bug that is not already listed as an issue, please report to xuehui.hu@kcl.ac.uk and Give us feedback!
